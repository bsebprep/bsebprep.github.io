# Bseb Prep  
### Smart & Reliable Bihar Board Exam Preparation

---

## About Bseb Prep

**Bseb Prep** is a dedicated Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) exam preparation platform designed especially for **Class 10th (Matric)** and **Class 12th (Inter)** students.

The platform focuses on **exam-oriented preparation**, helping students understand what actually matters in Bihar Board exams. Bseb Prep supports both **Hindi Medium and English Medium** learners and follows the **latest Bihar Board exam pattern**.

Bseb Prep is also available as an Android application on the Google Play Store.

---

## Features

- 📘 Previous Year Question (PYQ) PDFs
- 📚 Chapter-wise study materials
- 🎯 Objective-focused preparation (50% exam weightage)
- 📝 Chapter-wise quizzes
- 🔄 PYQs available in quiz mode
- 📂 Question bank with exam-level questions
- ✍️ Subjective notes for long-answer questions
- 🧠 Structured and smart learning approach
- 🎓 Designed strictly according to Bihar Board syllabus

Bihar Board exams emphasize both:

- Strong focus on concepts and accuracy
